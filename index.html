AS A ASSIGNMENT

Bringing forward new ways to cooperate
and organize in the internet age

What information can we trust?

Can we trust the press? Governments?
Big tech companies? Universities?
Random people on the internet? What happens
when these things become politicized?

There must be a way to organize information in a decentralized way, allowing communities to create their own reputation and governance systems, so that we can come to consensus and see the sum total of all human knowledge.

Together we can change how society is organized, put power into the hands of those who’ve earned it, and distribute resources and opportunity far and wide.
